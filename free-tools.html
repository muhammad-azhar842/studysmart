Top 10 Free Tools for Online Students

These free tools make studying and organizing your life as a student much easier:

  1. Notion: Organize notes, schedules, and tasks.
  2. Google Docs: Collaborative document editing and cloud storage.
  3. Grammarly: Grammar and writing assistant.
  4. Khan Academy: Free video tutorials and exercises.
  5. Quizlet: Make flashcards and study sets.
  6. Anki: Spaced repetition flashcard tool.
  7. Forest App: Helps you stay off your phone and focus.
  8. Coursera: Free courses from top universities.
  9. Wolfram Alpha: Solve math problems with step-by-step help.
  10. Canva: Make beautiful presentations or project covers.

Use these tools to boost your productivity and save time!
