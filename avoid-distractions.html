How to Avoid Distractions While Studying

Distractions can kill your productivity. Here are some ways to stay focused:

  • Find a quiet space: Avoid noisy rooms and study in a calm environment.
  • Turn off notifications: Use “Do Not Disturb” mode or apps like Forest.
  • Set specific goals: Know exactly what you want to finish in your study session.
  • Use time blocks: Limit study time to focused blocks, then rest.
  • Remove physical distractions: Keep your phone in another room.

Staying focused is a habit. With consistency, it becomes easier.
