7 Study Techniques That Actually Work

Struggling to retain what you study? These 7 proven study methods will help you learn smarter and retain more:

  1. Pomodoro Technique: Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat.
  2. Active Recall: Test yourself on what you’ve just studied. Don’t just re-read.
  3. Spaced Repetition: Review material over increasing intervals (use apps like Anki).
  4. Feynman Technique: Teach the topic to someone else or yourself in simple terms.
  5. Mind Mapping: Create visual diagrams to connect ideas and concepts.
  6. Practice Tests: Simulate real exam conditions regularly.
  7. Healthy Routine: Get proper sleep, eat well, and avoid cramming late at night.

Try mixing a few of these techniques to find what works best for you!
